PlayShare Support

Welcome to PlayShare support! We're here to help you get the most out of your game-sharing experience.

About PlayShare

PlayShare is a community platform that connects local gamers who want to share, trade, and exchange video games. Build your collection, create wishlists, and discover fellow gamers in your area who share your passion for gaming.

Getting Started

Creating an Account

  1. Open the PlayShare app
  2. Tap "Create new user"
  3. Enter your email address and create a password (or use the auto-generated secure password)
  4. Choose a unique nickname
  5. Enter your city to connect with local gamers

Adding Games

  1. Go to the Search/Database tab
  2. Search for games by title
  3. Tap a game to view details
  4. Choose to add it to:
    • Shared: Games you own and are willing to trade
    • Wishlist: Games you want to acquire
    • Collection: Games you own but aren't sharing

Finding Local Gamers

  1. Go to the Users tab
  2. Toggle to "Local Users"
  3. Tap a user to view their profile
  4. Use the context menu to view potential exchanges

Arranging Exchanges

  1. Tap a game from another user
  2. View potential trade options
  3. Connect via Telegram
  4. Meet safely in a public place

Frequently Asked Questions

General

Is PlayShare free to use?
Yes! PlayShare is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
How does PlayShare make money?
Currently, it does not monetize. We focus on building a great community.
What platforms are supported?
iOS (iPhone and iPad).

Privacy & Safety

What information is collected?
Email, city name, and your game lists. No precise location tracking or ad sharing.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Passwords are encrypted via Firebase Authentication and data is securely stored.
How do I stay safe?
  • Meet in public places
  • Bring a friend
  • Inform someone
  • Trust your instincts
  • Inspect games before trading
